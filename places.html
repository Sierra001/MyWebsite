Interesting places in Mauritius

Below we use place cards. Each card is a <article> with the class place-card. The same CSS is re-used for every card.

Le Morne Brabant

South-west coast

A mountain on a peninsula with stunning sea views. It is also a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Chamarel Seven Coloured Earth

South-west, near Chamarel village

A small area of sand dunes in different colours, created by volcanic activity.

Port Louis waterfront

Capital city

The Caudan Waterfront is a lively area with shops, restaurants and views of the harbour.

Île aux Cerfs

East coast

A small island with a bright lagoon, beaches and water activities.
