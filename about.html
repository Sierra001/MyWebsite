About this template

This small website is designed for students who are learning CSS. We keep the styles simple and use clear class names.

Some examples:

  • .container – keeps content centered with a max-width.
  • .place-card – used on every card in places.html.
  • .gallery-item – used on every image in the gallery.
  • .nav-link and .nav-link--active – show the current page in the menu.

You can give this project to students and ask them to:

  • Change the colours in css/style.css.
  • Add a new place card with its own image.
  • Create a new page (for example: food.html) and link it from the menu.
